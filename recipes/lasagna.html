Lasagna

Description

This dish is a saucy and cheesy meal that is great for pasta lovers. It's easy to put together and just throw in the oven!

It includes layers of noodles, cheese, and pasta sauce.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Crushed tomatoes
  • Tomato sauce
  • Tomato paste
  • Sugar
  • Seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Parmesan
  • Mozzarella
  • Ricotta
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
